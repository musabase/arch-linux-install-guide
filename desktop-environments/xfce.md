# XFCE on Arch Linux

XFCE is a lightweight and stable desktop environment that focuses on performance and simplicity.

## Why XFCE
- Very low resource usage
- Ideal for older or low-end hardware
- Predictable and stable behavior

## Usage Notes
- Excellent choice for performance-focused systems
- Minimal visual distractions
